Delete personal notes or motivational contents

The application allow users to delete their personal notes/motivational contents. The good thing with this functionality is that users can delete note/motivational contents that they dont like wherever and whenever they want, the changes cause by deleting personal/motivational contents will be reflected to the application in real-time. Therefore, users does not need to refresh the application every time they committed such changes.
